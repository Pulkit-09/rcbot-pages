RC Bot — Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. What We Collect

RC Bot collects health and fitness data including nutrition logs, gym sessions, weight, and Whoop biometric data (recovery, HRV, sleep, strain) solely for the purpose of personal health tracking.

2. How We Use Your Data

All data is used exclusively to provide personalized health insights to you. Data is stored in your own Google Sheet and is never shared with third parties.

3. Whoop Data

RC Bot accesses Whoop data via the official Whoop API on your behalf. This includes recovery scores, sleep data, strain, and HRV. This data is only accessible by you.

4. Data Storage

Data is stored in your personal Google Sheets account. No data is stored on our servers beyond what is necessary to operate the bot.
